# MA MALNU KANANGA - BRAND GUIDE (ONE PAGE)

## LOGO & VISUAL IDENTITY

### Color Palette
| Color Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|------------|----------|-------|
| Primary Green | `#0A704D` | Main elements, primary buttons |
| Secondary Gold | `#D4AF37` | Accents, highlights, secondary buttons |
| Accent Blue | `#1E3A8A` | Links, icons, interactive elements |
| Neutral Light | `#F5F5F5` | Backgrounds, content areas |
| Neutral Dark | `#333333` | Main text |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds |

### Typography
**Headings:** Playfair Display (Latin) + Amiri (Arabic)
- Elegant serif font for academic and Islamic identity
- Used for: Logo, hero banners, page titles

**Body Text:** Poppins
- Clean, modern sans-serif for readability
- Used for: Paragraphs, navigation, buttons

### Illustration & Photography Style
**Illustrations:**
- Flat design with subtle gradients
- Colors aligned with brand palette with gold accents
- Themes: Education, Islam, community

**Photography:**
- Natural, documentary style
- Composition: Rule of thirds, leading lines
- Lighting: Natural light, soft lighting
- Subjects: Student activities, school facilities, school events
- Filters: Slight warm tone for a friendly feel

## TONE OF VOICE

### Brand Voice Characteristics
- **Professional** yet **friendly**
- **Islamic** yet **modern**
- **Informative** yet **easy to understand**
- **Trustworthy** yet **not rigid**

### Language Guidelines
1. **Use Positive Language**
   - ✅ "Join us"
   - ❌ "Don't miss the opportunity"

2. **Friendly but Professional**
   - ✅ "We're happy to assist you"
   - ❌ "Hi!" or "Thank you for your attention"

3. **Clear and Specific**
   - ✅ "Registration open until June 30, 2024"
   - ❌ "Registration will close soon"

4. **Respect the Audience**
   - ✅ "Dear parents and prospective students"
   - ❌ "You" (too generic)
